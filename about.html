About TrainerDex Emerald


This fan-made project built by Group 5, TrainerDex: Emerald website is made to give players a simple and easy way to look up Pokémon and trainers found in the game. Our group wanted to create a quick-reference guide that organizes basic information in one place, especially for players who just want a fast way to check who appears in each area.

We know our website has its limitations. The information we currently provide—such as Pokémon names, types, and trainer listings is still very basic. We don’t yet include deeper details like levels, movesets, abilities, or battle strategies that are especially useful for challenge runs, Nuzlockes, or competitive-style planning.

But even with these flaws, our main purpose remains clear and it is to offer a simple starting point for players who need quick access to basic trainers and Pokémon's data, to help new players navigate the game more easily without feeling overwhelmed by too much information, and to lay the foundation for a more complete TrainerDex as we continue improving and expanding the site. This project is something our group built with the intention of growing it over time. The TrainerDex may be simple now, but we aim to keep developing it into a more detailed and helpful resource for every Pokémon Emerald player.

We also want to keep the spirit of playing Pokémon Emerald for the fans who still continue to enjoy playing for years after its release. This website is dedicated for our fellow fans of Pokémon GBA Games. We hope this becomes your trusted companion through your journey at Hoenn region, whether you're battling Gym Leaders, taking on Team Aqua/Magma, or preparing for the Pokémon League. Thank you for visiting, and happy training!

- Group 5
